AO Property

The AppObject to display.

Syntax
VB 
'Declaration
Public Property AO As imsAppObject

Remarks

This is how the AppObject is passed to the form.

Example

This example displays the sales order AppObject from the Sales Order Card.

VB 
Dim frmAO As New frmDisplayAppObject


With frmAO
    .AO = bdrSalesOrder.AO
    .Title = "Sales Order"
    .ShowAO()
End With
